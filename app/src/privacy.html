Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Welcome to NovaArcade. We value your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

We follow the 'minimum necessary' principle. We do not collect your personal identity information unless you voluntarily provide it.

Collected data is used only for providing services, improving gaming experience, and detecting fraud.
