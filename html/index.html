Privacy Protection Statement

We highly value the protection of your personal information. Our platform and games are committed to following the principle of minimal data collection:

  • Data Storage: Game progress and preference settings are by default saved only on your local device to ensure data security.
  • Information Collection: We promise not to actively collect sensitive privacy information such as your location, contacts, SMS records, etc.
  • Third-party Services: Encrypted transmission of the minimum necessary data occurs only when you use "Cloud Sync" or "Social Sharing" features.
